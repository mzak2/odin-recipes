Pizza Recipe
Our Signature Chilimac recipe is typically made in about 8 hours and requires a Crockpot. Alternatives can be found in grey sections.
Sign in to save or see other recipes!

First! The Chili ingredients...

  • 1 lbs. Ground Turkey
  • 1x can of Pinto Beans
  • 1x can of Dark Kidney Beans
  • 1x Can of Light Kidney Beans
  • 1x can of Black Beans
  • 2x Diced Bell peppers
  • 1x Diced Onion
  • 1x can of Corn Kernels
  • 1x can of Diced Tomatoes
  • 1x can of Tomato Paste
  • 1x packet of preferred Taco or Chili seasoning
  • 2x tbsp of salt
  • 2x tbsp of Pepper
  • 2x tbsp of Olive Oil

Making the Chili

  1. Place Ground Turkey, Diced Bell Peppers, Diced Onions, Salt, Pepper, and Olive oil into a pan
  2. Cook on Medium heat until turkey appears mostly done
  3. Grab your crockpot and add all ingredients into it, including everything canned and what you just cooked
  4. Stir and break up the meat if necessary in the crockpot until everything is mixed
  5. Place Crockpot on "LOW" for 8 Hours

Alternatively use a Can of store bought chili!

Sauce ingredients

  • 1x garlic clove
  • 1x can of tomato paste
  • 1x tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/2x teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1x tbsp sugar
  • 1x tbsp of salt
  • 2x tbsp of basil
  • 1/2x cup of finely diced white onion

Making the sauce

  1. dice up garlic, onions, basil, and oregano
  2. put tomato paste into a pan and begin warming at low heat
  3. add up to 1x cup of water to paste for consistency
  4. wait for paste mixturer to be warm but not boiling
  5. Mix in 1x tbsp of olive oil, 1x tbspsugar, 1x tbsp of salt
  6. Add your diced garlic, onions, basil, and oregano
  7. Stir for 10min

Want it easy? Instead use store
bought Macaroni and Cheese!

Mac n Cheese

  • 1x Ball of mozzarella (or pizza mix of cheese)
  • 1x Jimmy Dean Maple Sauasge roll
  • 16oz of Tomato sauce above (alternative Classico Hearty Tomato Basil)
  • 4x cups of Fresh spinach leaves or zucchini (diced if preferred)

Assembling the Pizza

  1. After letting dough rise for 3 hours, begin rolling it out into a square
  2. Place onto a Pizza Stone or Cookie Sheet with a olive oil rub to avoid sticking
  3. Use 1/2 of pizza dough to make desired shape
  4. Add sauce, should be thick enough to barely see the dough through it
  5. Use 1/2 ball of mozzarella after grading or dicing
  6. Place Spinach leaves to desired amount
  7. Use 1/2 of COOKED Jimmy Dean Maple Sausage
  8. Preheat oven to 425 degrees
  9. Place pizza on stone or cookie sheet into oven
  10. Cook for 13-15min
  11. Remove from oven, wait 10min, cut, and enjoy
