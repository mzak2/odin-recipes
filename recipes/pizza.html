Pizza Recipe

Dough ingredients

  • 4x cups of flour
  • 2x 1/2 cups of luke warm water
  • 3x tbsp of olive oil
  • 2x tbsp of sugar
  • 1x tbsp of salt

Making the dough

  1. Begin by placing 2x cups of flour into a mixing bowl
  2. Add 2x tbsp of sugar and 1x tbsp of salt
  3. Add the other 2x cups of flour
  4. turn on faucet and wait for water to get warm, it should feel slightly warm
  5. Add 2 1/2x cups of warm water to the bowl and begin mixing on Speed 1 (or kneading)
  6. Add 3x tbsp of olive oil and begin mixing
  7. Knead or mix for roughly 10min
  8. The dough should be wet enough to stick, but dry enough to keep form of a ball
  9. Use more flour or water to get the above consistency
  10. Place on countertop with plastic wrap or towel over bowl
  11. Allow to rise for 3 hours

Sauce ingredients

  • 1x small garlic clove (1/2 medium)
  • 1x can of tomato paste
  • 1x tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/2x teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1x tbsp sugar
  • 1x tbsp of salt
  • 2x tbsp of basil
  • 1/2x cup of finely diced white onion

Making the sauce

  1. dice up garlic, onions, basil, and oregano
  2. put tomato paste into a pan and begin warming at low heat
  3. add up to 1x cup of water to paste for consistency
  4. wait for paste mixturer to be warm but not boiling
  5. Mix in 1x tbsp of olive oil, 1x tbspsugar, 1x tbsp of salt
  6. Add your diced garlic, onions, basil, and oregano
  7. Stir for 10min

Toppings

  • 1x Ball of mozzarella (or pizza mix of cheese)
  • 1x Jimmy Dean Maple Sauasge roll
  • Tomato sauce above (alternative Classico Hearty Tomato Basil)
  • Fresh spinach leaves or zucchini (diced if preferred)

Assembling the Pizza

  1. After letting dough rise for 3 hours, begin rolling it out into a square
  2. Place onto a Pizza Stone or Cookie Sheet with a olive oil rub to avoid sticking
  3. Use 1/2 of pizza dough to make desired shape
  4. Add sauce, should be thick enough to barely see the dough through it
  5. Use 1/2 ball of mozzarella after grading or dicing
  6. Place Spinach leaves to desired amount
  7. Use 1/2 of COOKED Jimmy Dean Maple Sausage
  8. Preheat oven to 425 degrees
  9. Place pizza on stone or cookie sheet into oven
  10. Cook for 13-15min
  11. Remove from oven, wait 10min, cut, and enjoy
