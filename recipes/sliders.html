Pulled-Pork Sliders
A relatively easy meal made in a crockpot and perfect for parties, goes great with just about any BBQ sauce!
Sign in to save or see other recipes!

Go find yourself some Pork!

  • 2 lbs. Pork Shoulder/Loin
  • 4x tbsp. Worcestershire Sauce
  • 1x Chopped Onion
  • 4x Chopped Carrots
  • 1x Chopped Potatoe
  • 1x Favorite Rub (optional)

Prep the Pot

  1. Chop your Onions, Carrots, and Potatoe into large chunks and place in crockpot
  2. Rub down your Pork with your favorite rub and add 4x tbsp of Worcestershire Sauce
  3. Add your 1x can of Chicken broth
  4. Place Vegetables on bottom, put in your Pork Shoulder/ Loin, and cover.
  5. Place Crockpot on "LOW" for 10 Hours

Unfortunately I don't know a Vegetarian alternative!

The Sandwich supplies!

  • 1x Bag of Hamburger Buns (preferably Potatoe Bread)
  • 1x Bag of Fresh Tri-color Coleslaw salad mix (not the wet kind)
  • 1x Package of Slices of Cheddar or Colby-Jack Cheese
  • 1x Bottle of Sweet Baby Ray's BBQ sauce or your favorite
  • 3x tbsp. Siracha or some hot-sauce if you like

Pull it apart!

  1. After 10 hours, get yourself a big bowl, that can fit your Pork.
  2. With tongs or by hand (wait till it cools!), begin pulling pieces of the pork off and place in the bowl.
  3. Do so until all that is left in the Crockpot is the veggies and broth.
  4. Add a bunch of BBQ sauce, usually half a bottle.
  5. Begin mixing and stirring the pork should easily slider around string like.
  6. Mix in any additional sauces or seasonings.

Tacos or Flat-bread Sandwiches also
can be made with this pulled pork!

Assembling the Sliders!

  • 1x Bag of Hamburger Buns (preferably Potatoe Bread)
  • 1x Bag of Fresh Tri-color Coleslaw salad mix (not the wet kind)
  • 1x Package of Slices of Cheddar or Colby-Jack Cheese
  • 1x Bottle of Sweet Baby Ray's BBQ sauce or your favorite
  • 3x tbsp. Siracha or some hot-sauce if you like

Hopefully you can handle just one step in this one!

  1. Place your bun on even surface, add a helping of pork, then the Tri-Color Coleslaw, and finally Slices of Cheese!
